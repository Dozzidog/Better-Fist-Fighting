This mod that adds fist fighting from the Indiana Jones movie franchises.

**Why I decided to make this mod**

Its just clicking... thats literaly it clicking to hit a mob over and over and over agian. Thats why I created this mod this mod to make fist fighting or hand to hand combat more fun with dodges, takedowns, power punches, and more!

It also works with my other mod [Bullwhip Combat](https://github.com/Dozzidog/Bullwhip-Combat)!
